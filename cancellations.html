Cancellations & Refunds

At Royal Collections, we strive to ensure your shopping experience is seamless. Please read our cancellation and refund policies carefully.

1. Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order before it has been shipped. To cancel an order, please contact our customer support team immediately with your Order ID.

If the order has already been dispatched, we will not be able to cancel it. In such cases, you may refer to our Replacement Policy once the item is delivered.

2. Refunds

Refunds are applicable only in the following cases:

  • Cancellations before shipping: A full refund will be initiated to your original payment method.
  • Non-availability: If a product you ordered is out of stock and cannot be replaced, a full refund will be processed.
  • Defective/Wrong Item (if replacement is not possible): If a replacement is not available for a valid return claim, a refund will be provided.

3. Refund Processing Time

Once a refund is approved, it will be processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited back to the original source of payment (Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, or Net Banking).

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds (if applicable) will be transferred to your provided bank account via NEFT/IMPS.
