Replacement Policy

Royal Collections provides replacement facilities for ladies’ and kids’ garments, sandals, and fancy ornaments, subject to verification of the product condition. Customers must request a replacement within 2 days from the date of delivery, and requests made after this period will not be entertained.

All items eligible for replacement must be unused, unwashed, unworn, and in their original condition, along with original tags, packaging, and invoice. Any item that has been washed, altered, used, or damaged will not be accepted for replacement under any circumstances.

Inner garments are strictly non-replaceable and non-returnable due to hygiene and safety reasons.

Replacement of products purchased during sale, offer, or discounted periods will be considered only if the product meets all replacement conditions mentioned above. Replacement is subject to availability of the same product, size, color, or design; if the same item is unavailable, Royal Collections reserves the right to offer an alternative product or deny the request.

All replacement requests are processed only after quality inspection, and Royal Collections reserves the sole right to approve or reject any replacement request if the product does not meet the stated conditions.

Shipping Costs for Replacements

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item for replacement. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If an alternative product is sent to you, the cost of shipping that replacement item may be charged depending on the order value.

If the replacement is due to a manufacturing defect verified by our team, Royal Collections may bear the shipping costs at our discretion.

How to Initiate a Request

To initiate a replacement request within the 2-day window, please contact us immediately with your Order ID and clear photographs of the product condition.
